# University of Tennessee, Department of Economics

GitHub home for research code, data, and working papers from the
[Department of Economics](https://haslam.utk.edu/economics/) at the
University of Tennessee, Knoxville.

### Repositories

- **[repec](https://github.com/University-of-Tennessee-Econ/repec)** — the
  department's working paper archive, indexed on
  [IDEAS](https://ideas.repec.org/), [EconPapers](https://econpapers.repec.org/),
  and Google Scholar under the RePEc handle `RePEc:ten`.
